About

Kenner Swift writes techno-thrillers about the infrastructure American life rests on and what happens when that infrastructure is treated as a target rather than a given. His background is in technology, process design, and the operational realities of complex systems.

The Thousand Man War is his debut novel and the first volume of a planned trilogy. He lives in the northeastern United States with his wife.
